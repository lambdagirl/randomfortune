
 

No.4 GOOD FORTUNE

Although you hope to succeed in life, gods will not furnish you with income. If you
apply good manuscript to him, you will soon get income.

Then you can obtain good fortune repeatedly.

  

*Your wishes will be realized. *A sick person will recover in a little while, *The
person you are waiting for will come soon. *You should be careful in health all the
time during your trip. *Building a new house, removal, marriage and employment are
all good.

The lost article will be found but take a little while.
